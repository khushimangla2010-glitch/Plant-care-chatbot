A command-line chatbot that gives you personalized plant care advice based on your city's live weather. Built with Python.

What It Does?
Fetches live weather for any city
Detects your climate type (tropical, subtropical, temperate, cold, or dry)
Suggests plants suited to your current weather
Gives a full care schedule for any plant you ask about
Logs every search to plants.json so repeated lookups never hit the API again

How to Use?

Enter your city name when prompted
The bot shows live weather and detects your climate
Choose an option:

Option 1 - Get 5 plant suggestions suited to your weather
Option 2 - Type any plant name to get its full care schedule
Option 3 - Exit

Plant Database
30 plants across 5 categories are built into database.py

Each plant entry includes:
Watering frequency
Sunlight requirements
Soil type
Pruning schedule
Temperature range
Maintenance level
Growth rate
Indoor/outdoor suitability
